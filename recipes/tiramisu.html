Tiramisù

Description

Tiramisù is the quintessential Italian dessert, known worldwide for its creamy texture and bold coffee flavor.
This classic no-bake treat is made by layering espresso-soaked ladyfingers with a rich mascarpone cream,
lightly sweetened and whipped to perfection.
The final touch is a dusting of bitter cocoa powder, which balances the sweetness and enhances its depth of flavor.
This recipe is from the Italian web site: Giallo zafferano's .

Ingredients

  • Mascarpone cheese: 3 ⅓ cups (750 g)
  • Eggs: 5 (260 g) - (very fresh, medium)
  • Savoiardi (ladyfingers): 8.75 oz (250 g)
  • Sugar: ⅔ cup (120 g)
  • Coffe: 1 ⅓ cup (300 g) - (made in a moka pot, with sugar to taste)
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder to taste for decorating
