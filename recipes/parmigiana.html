Parmigiana

Description

The parmigiana di melanzane is a classic neapolitan recipe which has aubergines as its base.
There are a lot of variations of this recipe but I purpose the classic one;
for example there is the sorrentina's version in which the aubergines are breaded with eggs, flour and breadcrumbs.

Ingredients

  • Aubergines: 3
  • Mozzarella: 250 gr
  • Tomato souce: 700 gr
  • Garlic: 2 cloves
  • Basil: 4 leaves
  • Parmesan: 50 gr
  • Olive oil: 4 spoons
  • salt: to taste
