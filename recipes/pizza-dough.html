Pizza Dough

Description

This is the recipe of the famous neapolitan pizza dough from the AVPN

There are few steps to follow but very carefully.

Ingredients

  • Water:1 lt
  • Salt:from 40 to 60 gr
  • Yeast:
    • Fresh: 0.1-3 g
    • Dry: 1/3 fresh (e.g. 3 gr dry = 1 gr fresh)
    • Sourdough starter: 20 % of the Flour
  • Flour:from 1600 to 1800 gr

Steps

  1. Put water, salt and yeast in a bowl or mixer
  2. Start mixing to diluite salt and yeast in the water
  3. Put gradually the flour in about 10 minutes
  4. Continue kneading for about 20 minutes until the dough is smooth and homogeneous
  5. Let the dough rest (first fermentation) until it doubles in size
  6. Then proceed with the staglio (cutting) in loaves of 250 gr approx.
  7. Let the loaves rest (second fermentation) until the doubles in size
  8. Roll out the loaves one at a time (strictly by hand) into a disc with a thickness of about 0.25 cm (tutorial here)
  9. Put your fauvorite ingredients on the disc (e.g. tomato sause and mozzarella)
  10. Cook your pizza in the oven (wood-fired, gas, electric) with a temperature of min. 485Â° for 60-90 seconds
